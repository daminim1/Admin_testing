What I do

3D modeling

I specialize in crafting 3D models with an unwavering commitment to achieving the highest level of fidelity. My primary tool for such endeavors is Blender, a platform where I meticulously bring ideas to life through intricate and precise 3D modeling. My work revolves around capturing the essence of objects with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring an unparalleled level of realism and quality in every project.

Video editing

I proficiently employ DaVinci Resolve and After Effects in my video editing endeavors, consistently striving to bring each project as close to the original vision of the author as possible. With an unwavering commitment to precision and creativity, I meticulously refine and enhance visual elements, ensuring a seamless and impactful final product that resonates with the intended artistic vision.

VR/AR specialist

Having successfully completed the VR/AR Specialist course, I have significantly expanded my understanding of the immersive realms of VR, AR, XR, and everything in between. Proficient in setting up headsets and adept in the intricacies of crafting experiences, I bring a wealth of knowledge to the table. My expertise lies in creating tailored and impactful experiences that leverage cutting-edge technology to its fullest potential, ensuring a seamless and immersive journey for users.
